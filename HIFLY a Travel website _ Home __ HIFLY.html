About us

The travel company you always looked for!

HIFLY is a decade young, IATA - accredited travel company since 2013. It is registered under the Registrar of companies act India with its office in Chennai.
This company is driven by expertise and propelled with enthusiasm; our management team has a combined experience of more than 20 years in the airline and travel trade. We are now a company consistently growing in our base and reach.
Our ambition is to provide you solutions to all your travel needs with the best possible ease of process, travel comfort, and personal attention with the best possible pricing as well. Our team of experienced and efficient personnel is committed to taking care of all your travel requirements.
HIFLY gives you a seamless offering of everything that you as a traveler require - Tour Packages, hotels, visas/passports, Air Tickets, Cruise Bookings, and Travel Insurance.
